About Kalpak

Celebrating Innovation, Creativity, and Technical Excellence

What is Kalpak?

Kalpak is the flagship annual technical festival of Shree Siddheshwar Women's College of Engineering, celebrating innovation, creativity, and technical excellence. Our festival brings together brilliant minds from across the region to compete, collaborate, and showcase their talents in various technical and cultural events.

Our Mission

To create a platform that nurtures innovation, encourages technical excellence, and provides students with opportunities to showcase their skills and creativity.

Kalpak serves as a bridge between academic learning and real-world applications, fostering an environment where students can explore emerging technologies, participate in meaningful competitions, and network with industry professionals.

About Our College

Shree Siddheshwar Women's College of Engineering, Solapur

Shree Siddheshwar Women's College of Engineering, Solapur is a premier institution dedicated to providing quality technical education to women. Our college is committed to fostering innovation, excellence, and leadership among our students through rigorous academic programs and practical learning experiences.

With state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and industry partnerships, we prepare our students to become competent engineers and leaders in their respective fields. Our curriculum emphasizes both theoretical knowledge and hands-on skills, ensuring our graduates are well-equipped to meet the challenges of the modern technological landscape.
