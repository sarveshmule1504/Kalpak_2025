About Kalpak

Kalpak is the flagship annual technical festival of Shree Siddheshwar Women's College of Engineering, celebrating innovation, creativity, and technical excellence. Our festival brings together brilliant minds from across the region to compete, collaborate, and showcase their talents.

Through a diverse range of events spanning coding competitions, robotics challenges, web development contests, and entrepreneurship pitches, Kalpak provides a platform for students to push their boundaries and explore the frontiers of technology.

🎯

Our Mission

To create a platform that nurtures innovation, encourages technical excellence, and provides students with opportunities to showcase their skills and creativity. Kalpak acts as a bridge between academic learning and real-world application.

About Our College

Shree Siddheshwar Women's College of Engineering, Solapur is a premier institution dedicated to providing quality technical education to women.

Our college stands as a beacon of empowerment, nurturing the next generation of women engineers who will shape the future of technology and innovation. With state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and a commitment to excellence, we prepare our students to excel in the global technology landscape.
